Welcome

The internet is a powerful tool, connecting billions worldwide. However, it can also be misused to harm others. Cyberbullying is when someone uses technology—social media, messaging apps, online games, or email—to harass, threaten, or embarrass someone.

Rising Issue

Online harassment is increasing as more people spend time online.

Wide Impact

Teens, adults, and professionals can experience cyberbullying.

Emotional Effects

It can cause anxiety, depression, and low self-esteem.

About Cyberbullying

Cyberbullying involves intentional online behavior that harms another person.
